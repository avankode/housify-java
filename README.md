## Queify - What it does :
Blinkit , Swiggy Instamart and Zepto have certainly made our lives better. But those delivery charges man!
All of these platforms charge a delivery fee and if you order something worth say Rs.60 - you might as well most times still end up paying upwards of Rs.180 when the delivery charge gets added! 

https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2f63beb2-48c6-4dcb-ad9d-3e12fc08b4c2

#### With Queify - 
If you notice that you're out of milk and need it tomorrow , you can open the **Housify** app and add it to the queue . The second you're queue reaches a total that is more than the minium delivery charge discount amount , you will get notified on your GChat Housify group , with you and your flatmates. You can then simply place the order.

### You just saved yourself and your flatmates from paying absurd delivery charges.

### Expensify - 
Expenses around the house get difficult to track . Sure we have Splitwise to balance expenses amongst each other , but what happens when someone asks you "How much do you spend on the House per month?". Suddenly you realise that your Splitwise is cluttered with several external expenses too , even just amongst your roomates.

#### With Expensify -
A tab for expenses for all your Blinkit , Swiggy and Zepto orders , a  tab for rentals shared amongst you and your roomates (the Sofa, the TV, etc.) and finally , a tab for your personal house expenses ( your Bed, your study table ) , a centralised page , so that in one go , you know exactly what your monthly expense is. You can now plan better , and save better too!

### Laundrify
Theres one washing machine and 3 of us. You have a ton of laundry and no clean clothes for the next day at work. You take your basket to the washing machine only to realise that for the second day in a row , someone else is already using it. To make matters worse , Bangalore isn't known to be the best place in terms of clothes drying fast either .

#### With Laundrify -
Book your Laundry slot ,have the app gauge how long you'll have your clothes up on the drying rack according to the season ( clothes dry slower in the Monsoons ,a little fast in the Windters and much fast in the Summmers) and update the Group when the machine is free and who's slot it is next! No more having to call dibs , and no more pending Laundry!
